Equity Lab — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-15

Equity Lab (also listed as “Equity Lab: Poker Calculator”) is developed by lei cao ("we"). This policy explains what data the app handles and why.

What the app is

Equity Lab is an offline poker equity calculator. Hand ranges, board cards, saved scenarios, and settings live on your device. There is no Equity Lab backend and no user accounts.

What we collect

  • Usage analytics (Google Firebase Analytics): anonymous app-usage events such as calculation runs, range edits, preset applications, multi-board (ESG) runs, scenario saves, paywall views, and purchase outcomes, plus device identifiers used by Firebase. We use this to understand how the app is used and improve it. Event parameters do not include your hand cards, player names, or other personal content.
  • Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics): technical crash data (stack traces, device model, OS version) to fix bugs. Not linked to your identity.
  • Performance / diagnostic signals bundled with Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics (for example performance diagnostics) for app functionality and analytics. Not linked to your identity.
  • Purchase history (Apple App Store / Google Play): the one-time Pro unlock (`pro_unlock`) is processed by the store. The app uses the store’s purchase state to unlock features; we never see your payment card details.

    • Per App Store privacy categories (see also the app’s App Privacy Details): product interaction, crash data, performance data, other diagnostic data, and purchase history — for analytics and/or app functionality, and not linked to your identity.

    What we don't collect

    No accounts, no names, no emails, no contacts, no location, no photos, no messages, no advertising identifiers for ads. Your calculator state and saved scenarios are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to an Equity Lab server (there is none). The app does not show ads and does not use App Tracking Transparency or a UMP ad-consent form.

    No ads / no tracking for advertising

    Equity Lab has no ads. We do not use your data to track you across other companies’ apps or websites for advertising.

    Purchases

    The one-time “Equity Lab Pro” unlock is processed entirely by Apple App Store / Google Play. We never see your payment details. Restore purchases uses the same store APIs.

    Your choices

  • Use the app fully offline for calculations; network is only needed for Firebase diagnostics/analytics and for store purchases/restore.
  • Delete saved scenarios and clear app data from the device to remove local content.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local storage.

    • Children

    Equity Lab is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.
